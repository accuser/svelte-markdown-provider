## Text directive

Text directives are inline directives that wrap text. They can be used to apply styles to text, such as highlighting.

`A :hl[text directive] is an :hl[inline]{bg=pink} directive.`

A :hl[text directive] is an :hl[inline]{bg=pink} directive.
